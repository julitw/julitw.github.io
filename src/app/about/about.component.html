Welcome to our Angular application that displays air pollution statistics from various locations around the world.

Map Legend:

  • Good
  • Fair
  • Moderate
  • Poor
  • Very Poor

Click on a location on the map to determine its coordinates. You can name this location and add it to the list of monitored places.

Comparative charts will be generated for locations marked on the map or in the table. The list of places is editable.

You can sort data by clicking on the column header and filter using sliders to specify the minimum and maximum values for each pollutant.
